## Problem ❓
Product managers often receive a large amount of raw user feedback from surveys, support tickets, interviews, and app reviews.
Turning this raw input into clear themes, pain points, and actionable priorities is slow and messy.

## Target User 🎯
- Product Managers
- Product Analysts
- Founders
- Growth teams

## Goal 🎉
Help PMs convert raw feedback into:
- themes
- pain points
- feature requests
- urgency signals
- suggested priorities

## MVP 🏅
Version 1 should do only these:
1. Receive raw feedback text
2. Group similar feedback
3. Extract main pain points
4. Highlight repeated requests
5. Suggest simple priority labels

## Why this matters Ⓜ️
PMs spend too much time reading scattered feedback.
This tool helps them move faster from input to decision.

## Future Ideas 🔮
- sentiment detection
- tagging by persona
- export to PRD
- trend comparison by time
