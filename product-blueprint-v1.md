# Product Blueprint v1 — SignalForge

## Product name
SignalForge

## Product category
AI-assisted product operations platform

## Core promise
SignalForge reduces the distance between user feedback and execution.

## Problem
Product teams collect feedback from many channels, but turning that information into actionable reports, standardized product documents, and execution-ready tasks is slow, inconsistent, and manual.

## Target users
- Product Managers
- Product Operations teams
- Founders
- Product Analysts
- Research-enabled product teams

## Core workflow
1. Collect feedback through forms, surveys, links, or imports
2. Ingest structured and unstructured inputs
3. Analyze feedback into themes, pain points, and opportunities
4. Generate reports and summaries
5. Convert insights into a standardized PRD draft
6. Generate execution-ready tasks for delivery

## Product modules
- SignalForge Collect
- SignalForge Insights
- SignalForge PRD Studio
- SignalForge Tasks

## Current focus
SignalForge Insights

## MVP v1
The first version should allow users to:
- input raw text feedback
- import basic structured data
- generate structured insights
- generate a summary report
- produce a PRD draft
- produce an initial task list

## Out of scope for v1
- advanced survey distribution
- real SMS integration
- complex permissions
- team collaboration
- advanced workflow automation
- external integrations

## Strategic value
SignalForge is not just a form builder, survey tool, dashboard, summarizer, or PRD generator. It connects the workflow from feedback to execution.
